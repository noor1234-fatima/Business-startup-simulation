About StartupSim

StartupSim was built as a university project to help students explore entrepreneurship through hands-on micro-simulations. It blends creativity, strategy, and fun to teach core business concepts.

Purpose

Teach decision-making: allocate budget, choose team, and understand trade-offs in launching a startup.

Who it's for

Students, early-stage entrepreneurs, and project groups looking to explore and present startup ideas.

Built with

HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript (no backend required). LocalStorage used for persistence.
